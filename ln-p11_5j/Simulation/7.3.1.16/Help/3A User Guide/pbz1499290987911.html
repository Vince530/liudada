Tune EV gain cap

Exposure value (EV) settings extend the digital gain and could cause high gain. There are optional tuning methods to control and specify how the EV adjusts the exposure table.

By default, the gain extends freely under EV.

  1. On the AECtab, select the EV Adjustment check box.
  2. Select the EV Gain Capping check box.
  3. Enter the preferred value, greater than the gain value from the final knee point.
    The gain value is capped when the EV exceeds this value.
