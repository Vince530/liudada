Tune AF SAD parameters threshold min and threshold max

Threshold_min and threshold_max are the same AF SAD parameters used for contrast AF.

In general, SAD is used to detect scene changes when the laser sensor cannot detect a scene change in the far distance (60 cm and beyond). Ideally, the SAD for TOF is less sensitive compared to contrast AF. If it is not less sensitive, unnecessary AF triggering is introduced.
