Advanced AEC tuning procedures

Advanced camera tuning is real-life, scene-based tuning that fully optimizes the camera AEC. Advanced camera tuning is optional, but is recommended for optimal camera performance.

It is not recommended to tune preflash.

  • Tune dynamic metering for bright and dark regions
  • Tune extreme color detection and luma adjustment
  • Tune histogram AEC luma target
  • Tune flash and face detection
  • Preflash tuning
  • LED measurement
  • Dual LED calibration
  • Tune dual camera AEC
