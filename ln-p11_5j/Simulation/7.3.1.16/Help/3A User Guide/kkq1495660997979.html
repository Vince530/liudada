Tune TAF ROI

After CAF ROI is tuned, tune the TAF ROI to focus on smaller objects.

TAF should have a smaller ROI size than the CAF ROI. The variable is a scaling factor of the CAF ROI. For example, a default value of 0.6 for the touch scaling factor parameters applied to a 0.16666 clip_ratio, results in a TAF clip ratio of 0.6 * 0.1666 = 0.0966.

Increase/decrease scaling factor by 0.1 until TAF achieve desire success rate.

Note: To avoid dependency on the algorithm, you can enable the full-sweep search when tuning ROI.
