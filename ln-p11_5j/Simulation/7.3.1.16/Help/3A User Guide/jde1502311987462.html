Dominant face AEC

Face ROI tuning is the luma metering blending of face and frame Luma.

Legacy face AEC allows a single weight between face ROIs and frame to specify the blending of frame and face luma. When a strong weight for face luma is chosen, the frame can be overexposed due to heavy compensation of face luma, even if the face is a small area. Dominant face AEC interpolates the face weight based on the face size to choose a more suitable weight to avoid vigorous compensation. In general, the tuning value of minimum face weight (start), which corresponds to minimum face size (start), should be a small value to result in mild compensation. For maximum face weight (end), the value should be higher (stronger) to ensure more stable face luma, as the face becomes the dominant salient object in the FOV.
