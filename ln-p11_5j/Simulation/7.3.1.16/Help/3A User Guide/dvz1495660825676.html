Histogram AEC luma target

The tuning parameters for histogram AEC luma target adjustment are generic and not sensor dependent. They do not need to be retuned whenever a new sensor is enabled. Tuning is only required to fine-tune and match reference, or to prevent stability issues. Do not change any of the parameters unless you are certain of its effect.

In addition, the feature is not fully compatible with many manual features, such as EV/brightness. It can also modify the behavior of touch AEC and face AEC, and in particular, in certain cases, touch AEC can be back-compensated by this feature, and underexposure which originally could happen due to touching a bright area may end up being very mild or not happening at all. Disable manual EV, etc., in order to enable this feature. Behaviors can be abnormal (like touch AEC) if features not compatible with histogram AEC are forced to run together with histogram AEC.

The histogram luma target adjustment allows for the following:

  • Increasing the luma target to achieve the tunable percent of saturated pixels
  • Capping the max luma target offset
