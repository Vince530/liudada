Tune time-of-flight (TOF) AF

TOF assisted AF is a feature that helps to speed up AF. In TOF AF, the laser sensor measures the distance to the target by calculating the time between emitting an infrared light towards the target and receiving the infrared light back at the receiver. The distance between the camera and the object is calculated based on the trip time and the speed of light. The accuracy of the calculation relies on the accuracy of the contrast-based AF tuning.
