Required AEC tuning procedures

The following AEC tuning procedures are required as part of the camera tuning process.

  • Tune the exposure table
  • Tune AEC lux index normalization
  • Tune flash exposure tables
  • Tune AE luma target
  • Tune flash triggers
  • Tune ISO value
  • Capture images for LED flash tuning
  • Tune LSC for LED flash
  • Tune flux and color ratios
