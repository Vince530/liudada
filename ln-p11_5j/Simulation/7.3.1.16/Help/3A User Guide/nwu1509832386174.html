FA SA computation of confidence level

The FA SA module computes a confidence level that the decision aggregator can use.

The decision aggregator interpolates the target points from each scene analyzer based on their confidence level. One of the interpolated target points is from the FA SA.

The FA SA target point confidence level is computed by multiplying two sub-confidence levels. Each sub-confidence level is defined with a two-dimensional trigger. An example scenario for computing the final confidence level of face-assist AWB target point using the two sub-confidence levels is shown in the illustration below.

  • Confidence level 1 triggers are:
    • Lux level
    • Face stats percentage of total stats
  • Confidence level 2 triggers are:
    • Regular CCT
    • Face stats percentage of total stats
