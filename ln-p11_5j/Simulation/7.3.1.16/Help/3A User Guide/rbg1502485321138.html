Tune flash and face detection

Flash and face detection (FD) leverages the face detection feature and the dominant face AEC feature and extends them to the flash snapshot.

  1. Set up a test scene with a mannequin (not a paper photo) that is easily detected by the FD feature in bright light.
  2. Reduce the amount of light in the scene to trigger the flash.
  3. In the Parameter Editor, navigate to 3A > AEC > Flash > Flash Ext > Flash FD Request Delay Lux Trigger and adjust the start and end values as needed.
  4. Navigate to 3A > AEC > Flash > Flash Mtr > FD Flash and enable FD flash and adjust parameters as needed.
