Weight FCM parameters for AWB dual camera sync

The weight FCM parameters are used to determine the ratio for mixing GCM and FCM for use in HCM mode.

In general, the use of GCM is recommended. Because GCM compares the average ratio of the gray stats in the GCM region between the master camera and the slave camera, it is less likely to be misjudged because it does not use a fixed target point like FCM does. Also GCM is stronger than FCM with respect to module-to-module deviation.
