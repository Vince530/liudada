Verify dual camera AEC

After tuning dual AEC, verify improvements.

  1. After tuning, test the Dual Cam Exp Comp Multiplier.
    1. Go to > Parameter Editor > 3A > AEC > Extension > Dual Cam > Dual Cam Exp Comp Multiplier and update Dual Cam Exp Comp Multiplier with the tuned value.
    2. Set all CCT Based Comp to 1.0 as not to apply CCT-based adjustments. Go to Parameter Editor > 3A > AEC > Extension > Dual Cam > CCT Based Exp Comp and set the Compensation Ration of each array to 1.0.
    3. Capture one raw image per camera and open in Chromatix 7 to measure the average luma.
      The luma should be improved (i.e., closer) compared to before tuning.
  2. Test the CCT Based Comp.
    1. Go to Utilities > Parameter Editor > 3A > AEC > Extension > Dual Cam > CCTBased Exp Comp and complete the tuning array for CCT and Compensation Ratio values.
    2. Capture one raw image per camera and open in Chromatix 7 to measure the average luma.
