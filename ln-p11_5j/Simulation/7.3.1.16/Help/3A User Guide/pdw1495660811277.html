Capture images for LED flash tuning

Before tuning the LED flash for lens rolloff, capture raw images using two devices. In this example, the devices are referred to as device A and device B, where device A captures images lit by device B.

Capture images for lens rolloff LED1 and LED2

  1. Turn off any outside light sources (i.e., overhead lights).
  2. Place device A facedown with the lens covered with a diffuser filter.
  3. Place device B roughly 6 inches above device A.
  4. Capture one raw image per LED (one for single LED, two for dual LED):
    1. Use device A to capture one image illuminated only by LED1 of device B by covering LED2 of device B with black tape.
    2. Use device A to capture one image illuminated only by LED2 of device B by covering LED2 of device B with black tape.
