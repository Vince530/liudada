Preview ISO 100 gain

The higher the ISO value, the more sensitive the camera sensor. This allows higher shutter speeds under lower light conditions to avoid motion blur of captured images. However, the higher sensitivity can also result in higher background noise. As noise reduction is applied, Preview ISO 100 gain becomes less important in terms of noise. Some noise reduction algorithms reduce noise more aggressively at high gains, making the noise profile nonlinear. Preview ISO 100 gain becomes more relevant for motion blur reduction as higher ISO settings are manually configured by the camera user.
