Test actuator driver



The procedures in this section test the actuator and lens module while also verifying the parameters generated in the AF Tuning tab. The procedures can also be used for debugging. A successful completion of these procedures indicates that:

  • Lens module and actuator performance meet expectations
  • Actuator settings and OTP data are correct
  • Parameters generated in the AF Tuning tab are valid
Note:

All images used during the tuning process must be captured with the device that is being tuned.

The orientation of the device must be landscape when capturing test images.
