Tune CAF ROI

To tune CAF ROI, start with the default CAF settings and do the following:

  1. Center and place a small object at around 20 cm.
  2. Observe how many times the AF focuses on the object.
  3. Retrigger CAF and let it settle.
  4. Log the lens position.
  5. Repeat Steps 3-4 10 times.
  6. Verify that the lens position is always the same.
  7. Do one of the following:
    1. If the failure rate is high, then reduce the ROI size. Reducing CAF ROI size may cause a normal scene to fail.
    2. If the failure rate for a normal scene is not acceptable, increase the ROI size in increments of 0.1 from the default value.
