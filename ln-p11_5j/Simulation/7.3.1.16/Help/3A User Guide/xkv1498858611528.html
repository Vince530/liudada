Tune Bayer grid subsampling

Bayer grid (BG) statistics can be subsampled to reduce power consumption.

Subsampling patterns include:
  • Off – No subsampling
  • SW2 – Subsampling with a factor of 2
  • SW4 – Subsampling with a factor of 4

When subsampling is enabled, effective stats points are reduced depending on the subsampling factor.

By default, full stats are generated and accessed for calculations.

  1. In the Parameter Editor, navigate to 3A > AEC > Metering > Mtr Luma Calc > Index Calc > Bayer Grid Subsmpl Select.
  2. In the Value list, select the subsampling pattern.
  3. Verify that no degradation is present by comparing luma in the debug data from the same scene between one with no subsampling enabled vs. one with the subsampling pattern selected.
