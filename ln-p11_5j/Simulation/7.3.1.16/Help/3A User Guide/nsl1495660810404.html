Extreme color adjustment

Extreme red, blue, and green regions are detected and used to adjust AEC luma target.

Parameter Description Range Default
extreme_color _enable Use this flag to enable/disable the extreme color metering feature.

0 (disable)

1 (enable)

 1 (enable)
luma_low_th If the region luma for an extreme color region is smaller than this threshold, the region is weighted less in the extreme color feature. 1 to 255 30
luma_high_th If the region luma for an extreme color region is larger than this threshold, the region is weighted less in the extreme color feature. 1 to 255 225
color_stats_pct_low_th Minimum percentage of extreme color regions considered in each image frame. 0.0 to 1.0 0.05
color_stats_pct_high_th Maximum percentage of extreme color regions considered in each image frame. 0.0 to 1.0 0.5
adj_ratio
  • The adjustment ratios for R/G/B channels used to generate the decrease ratio of the luma_target.
  • Provide individual adjustment ratios for each channel/lux zone combination.
  • The smaller the adjustment ratio, the bigger the effect.
 0.5 to 1.0

{0.8, 0.9, 0.8} for Zone 0

{0.9, 0.9, 0.9} for Zone 1

{1.0, 1.0, 1.0} for Zone 2
green_rg_th
  • The R/G ratio threshold that is used with the B/G ratio threshold and the distance threshold to (1, 1) in R/G and B/G space to detect extreme green.
  • The interpolation zone of this threshold is given by green_rg_interp_pct.
  • The bigger the threshold, the more regions are detected as extreme green.
 0.8 to 1.2 1.1
green_rg_interp_pct
  • Interpolation percentage that is used with green_rg_th for extreme green detection.
  • The bigger the value, the bigger the interpolation range.
  • Constraint: green_rg_th*(1+green_rg_interp_pct) < 1.2
 0.0 to 0.2 0.05
green_bg_th
  • The B/G ratio threshold that is used with the R/G ratio threshold and the distance threshold to (1, 1) in R/G and B/G space to detect extreme green.
  • The interpolation zone of this threshold is given by green_bg_interp_pct.
  • The bigger the threshold, the more regions are detected as extreme green.
 0.8 to 1.1 0.95
green_bg_interp_pct
  • Interpolation percentage that is used with green_bg_th for extreme green detection.
  • The bigger the value, the bigger the interpolation range.
  • Constraint: green_bg_th*(1+green_bg_interp_pct) < 1.0
 0.0 to 0.2 0.05
green_ctr_th
  • The distance threshold to (1, 1) in the R/G and B/G space, that is used with R/G and B/G ratio thresholds to detect extreme green.
  • The interpolation zone of this threshold is given by green_cntr_interp_pct.
  • The smaller the threshold, the more regions are detected as extreme green.
 0.05 to 0.3 0.15
green_ctr_interp_pct
  • Interpolation percentage that is used with green_cntr_th for extreme green detection.
  • The bigger the value, the bigger the interpolation range.
  • Constraint: green_cntr_th*(1-green_cntr_interp_pct) > 0.05
 0.0 to 0.2 0.05
red_rg_th
  • The ratio threshold that is used with red_interp_pct to detect extreme red.
  • The smaller the threshold, the more regions are detected as extreme red.
 1.0 to 4.0 1.9
red_rg_interp_pct
  • The interpolation percentage that is used with red_th to detect extreme red.
  • The bigger the value, the bigger the interpolation range.
  • Constraint: red_th*(1-red_interp_pct) > 1.05
 0.0 to 0.5 0.07
blue_rg_th
  • Threshold that is used with blue_interp_pct to detect extreme blue.
  • The smaller the threshold, the more regions are detected as extreme blue region.
 1.0 to 2.0 1.2
blue_rg_interp_pct
  • Interpolation percentage that is used with blue_th to detect extreme blue.
  • The bigger the value, the bigger the interpolation range.
  • Constraint: blue_th*(1-blue_interp_pct) > 1.05
 0.0 to 0.5 0.07
