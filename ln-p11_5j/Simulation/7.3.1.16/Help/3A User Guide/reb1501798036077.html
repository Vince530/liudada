AEC camera startup

AEC startup tuning converges the preferred exposure at the beginning of a camera session as quickly as possible.

Fast AEC uses a high frame rate mode to quickly converge before switching to the preferred mode. Aggressive AEC allows for aggressive convergence. Generic startup tuning is fine tuning of convergence parameters to achieve faster convergence at startup only.

Fast AEC and aggressive AEC share the same convergence process to converge aggressively. Both fast AEC and aggressive AEC use an aggressive first frame heuristic that makes the first exposure adjustment larger when there are saturated stats.
