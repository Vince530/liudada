Fine tune AWB dual camera sync convergence speed

Prerequisite: Complete the initial AWB tuning process for each camera in its own Chromatix project. In the initial AWB tuning, measure the regular reference points of both cameras under the same light condition.

Fine tune convergence speed

If color tone peaking is severe after switching due to an incorrect transition AWB result, reduce the convergence speed.

  1. In the Parameter Editor, navigate to 3A > AWB > Dual Cam Sync (V1) > Conv Speed
  2. In the Value field, enter a new convergence speed.
  3. Click File > Save to project.
