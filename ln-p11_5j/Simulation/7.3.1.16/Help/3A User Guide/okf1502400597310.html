Dual LED calibration

Due to variation in LED modules, per-unit calibration is recommended for dual LED solutions. Per-unit calibration improves AWB consistency for zero lux photos and helps match ambient light sources. Per-unit calibration requires a fixture to isolate the device from ambient light on the production floor. As a prerequisite, tune the flux and color ratios.

Calibration is adjusted via the following parameters. Some basic settings are automatically populated by flux and color ratio tuning, but further tuning is required.
