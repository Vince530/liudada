Touch AEC

Touch AEC modifies the luma metering weight based on the specific region touched on the frame. The ROI Weight parameter changes the weight between the touch ROI region and the background.

AEC frame luma is calculated using the following formula:

LumaMetered = LumaTouch * WtTouch + LumaFrame *(1 - WtTouch)

Dynamic ROI

When the touch area is too bright or dark, images may be overexposed or underexposed when the tuned ROI weight is high. After touching the dark area, although the touch ROI becomes correctly exposed, everything else surrounding the ROI can become too dark to be visible. In the dynamic ROI metering feature, the histogram AEC tuning parameters are adjusted to aggressively prevent those over/under exposure scenarios. Upon touch being active, histogram tuning parameters are adjusted by bright and dark luma references, and min/max adjust ratios. The adjustments depend on the luma ratios between touch ROI and the frame.
