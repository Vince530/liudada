Capture outdoor landscape and portrait sequences

Scene File format Requirements ANR calibration TF calibration MF calibration
Outdoor landscape Image sequence
  • Requires at least 10 frames
  • Scene must include moving people/cars (occupying up to 20% FOV)
  • Exposure time up to 100 ms with OIS; up to 33 ms without OIS
  • No tripod
 NA NA Process with BPS, with rolloff enabled
Outdoor portrait Image sequence
  • Requires at least 10 frames
  • Scene must include people (occupying up to 30% FOV)
  • People should try not to move
  • Background should have details
  • No tripod
 NA NA Process with BPS, with rolloff enabled

Total # of images to capture = # of images to capture per lighting condition * # of lighting conditions. For most spatial modules, use six regions to cover bright to low light conditions.
