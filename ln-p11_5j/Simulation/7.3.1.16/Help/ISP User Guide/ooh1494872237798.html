Capture black level images

Related tuning block: Linearization

Capture two raw images in a completely dark room with the lens covered by a dark object.

  1. Under each lighting condition, let the camera’s AEC stabilize.
  2. Turn off AEC.
  3. Turn off all lights in the test room and capture a raw image with the lens covered with a dark object (e.g., black tape).
  4. Turn on AEC.
  5. Repeat Steps 1 through 4 with all light conditions.
